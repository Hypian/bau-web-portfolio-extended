About Us

MISAR, the Medical Imaging Students Association Rwanda, is a dynamic and dedicated organization comprised of passionate students pursuing careers in the field of medical imaging. Our association is driven by a shared vision of promoting excellence in medical imaging education, professional development, and community engagement. As future healthcare professionals, we are committed to making a meaningful impact on the healthcare sector through our collective efforts.

Mission

Values


  • Our vision is to be a leading force in shaping the future of medical imaging. We aspire to create a network of driven students and professionals, working collaboratively to enhance healthcare services, advocate for excellence in medical imaging, and improve the overall well-being of our communities.

  • Our Mission At MISAR, our mission is to empower students in the field of medical imaging by providing opportunities for academic and professional growth. We aim to foster a supportive community and contribute to the advancement of medical imaging practices, both locally and on a global scale.
  • The Medical Imaging Students Association Rwanda, is guided by the values of excellence, inclusivity, community engagement, professional development, and collaboration. These principles underpin our mission to positively impact healthcare in Rwanda.
